Welcome to the tutorial!

Here I will go over a few things to keep in mind when using CysMaps!


How do I make a map?

Well, I wont teach you how to make one from scrach, to figure that out consider consulting a helpful youtibe video, genarate your own with an online solution, or find a free to use one.
Once you have your map, it might be a tad small. Sizing it up will do no good, so I reccomend using a program like Inkscape to vectorize your map. Simply trace your map and save it in the desired size!

How do I cut my map up?

Either find your own tile-cutting service, there is one for photoshop, tutorial here. If you dont have Photoshop (me too buddy), use my my tilecutter! It requres vs-code to run, but that preogram is free, as opposed to Photoshop. Make sure you read my tile-cutter tutorial carefully even if you go the Photoshop route! You will still need to adhere to the rules I set up there!

I have my tile map, now what do I do?

Simply zip the folder housing the tiles, call this folder "map_tiles"!!!!, name the zip folder your desired map name, and upload it in the map manager!

I dont want to make my own map, I just want to try out CysMaps

Thats fine
I made a generic map for you to use, simply access that from the map manager and you can now play around with all the features I made!

What can I do with my map?

I have thus far only implemented the ability to add markers to your map! But they already offer a million options in regards to building your world!
